Web Development Course Summit 2021

The Global Summit brings together the community of exports, academics, and activists who comprise the Web Dev Course in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge- free software advocates, wikipedians. Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant softwares together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about CC Global Summit 2015!
